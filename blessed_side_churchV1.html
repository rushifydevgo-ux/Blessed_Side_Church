Our Mission
We Are A
Presence-
Driven
Church.

Taking the message of reconciliation to the world — that's not just what we do. It's who we are. At The Blessed Side, the Holy Spirit is the main attraction, and every gathering is a chance to encounter the living God.

We are a family. A tribe. A community that does life together, walks in faith together, and believes nobody gets left behind.

"When you walk in to the end of service you are Truly BLESSED. Worship and the message is definitely led by the Holy Spirit. You for sure feel like you're a part of a much larger family." — Church Member

Core Values
12 Ways
We Live.
01
Unity
"We're better together"
We believe the collective is greater than the individual. United we stand, divided we fall. In this house, no one gets left behind. Unity is the only thing that commands God's blessing.
"How good and pleasant it is when God's people live together in unity!" — Psalm 133:1
02
Servant-Leadership
"Every leader is a servant first"
Serving is a privilege. If you're too big to serve then you're too small to lead. We fill gaps whenever we see them. We serve willingly, cheerfully and joyfully.
"Whoever wants to become great among you must be your servant." — Mark 10:43
03
Forgiveness
"Unforgiveness is bad for your health"
Forgiveness is non-negotiable. We are commanded to extend mercy as we have been shown mercy. Forgiveness is not for them — it's for you. There can be no growth without forgiveness.
"Forgive anyone who offends you. Remember, the Lord forgave you." — Colossians 3:13
04
Authenticity
"Keep it real"
We don't do fake. We honor vulnerability, transparency and honesty. Letting go of who we think we're supposed to be and embracing who God called us to be.
"Let us not love with words or speech but with actions and in truth." — 1 John 3:18
05
Excellence
"Nothing less than our best"
By making the most of what we have, we represent the Lord by giving Him our best and trust that He will do the rest.
"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord." — Colossians 3:23
06
Generosity
"We don't have to, we get to"
We honor God by giving our first and our best. A spirit of generosity ministers to the heart of the Father. We are blessed to be a blessing and cheerfully give of our time, energy and effort.
"God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7
07
Communication
"We're not mind-readers"
We communicate clearly, openly, honestly and truthfully. Without communication there can be no trust. We are compassionate in tone and confront with grace.
"Everyone should be quick to listen, slow to speak and slow to become angry." — James 1:19
08
Honor
"The pathway to blessing"
Honor is foundational in the kingdom of God. Honor is the vehicle to blessing. We esteem those above us, around us and alongside us. We honor often, joyfully and loudly.
"Be devoted to one another in love. Honor one another above yourselves." — Romans 12:10
09
Stewardship
"We may hold it but we don't own it"
We steward our resources as gifts that God has entrusted to us. Everything we have is His — and we hold it with open hands and grateful hearts.
"The earth is the Lord's, and everything in it." — Psalm 24:1
10
Reconciliation
"Bring it to the table"
Reconciliation is the work of God. God has restored us — therefore it is our responsibility to initiate reconciliation with others. We reconcile vertically and horizontally. We are called to be Peacemakers.
"God through Christ reconciled us to himself and gave us the ministry of reconciliation." — 2 Cor. 5:18
11
Encouragement
"Say It Again!"
We believe in encouraging each other and aren't shy about showing it. We celebrate constantly and make everything a party. We reinforce individual excellence by banding together.
"Encourage one another and build up one another." — 1 Thessalonians 5:11
12
Community
"A Tribe Called Blessed"
At The Blessed Side, all who come are family. We constantly create space to connect with others. We move as a tribe. We cannot take the steps for you but we will take them with you.
"Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another." — John 13:34
How We Live
Culture
& Presence.

These aren't just ideas. They're the way we breathe, lead, and love at The Blessed Side.

Cloud Chasers

Presence-Driven

We prioritize and give the Holy Spirit the entire room — not just space. At The Blessed Side the Holy Spirit is the main attraction. Daily private encounters preface public encounters.

"For all who are being led by the Spirit of God, these are sons of God." — Romans 8:14
Pray, Obey, Repeat

Obedience & Trust

A lifestyle of obedience is necessary. We exercise our love of Jesus by obeying His word. Obedience honors and moves the heart of God. We honor a lifestyle of alignment to the Word.

"If you love me, you will obey my commands." — John 14:15
We Need Us All

Empowerment

We empower those with our heart to lead with their mind. We trade empowerment for teachability and humility. Every person entrusted to our care matters deeply.
